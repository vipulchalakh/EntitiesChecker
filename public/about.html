About Us

Learn about our mission, our team, and the technology behind EntitiesChecker

Our Mission

At EntitiesChecker, we're passionate about making advanced natural language processing accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide powerful, user-friendly tools that help people extract meaningful insights from web content.

Founded in 2023, our team of NLP experts and software engineers has developed a cutting-edge entity extraction system that can identify and categorize named entities from any public webpage with remarkable accuracy.

Whether you're a researcher, content creator, SEO specialist, or just curious about the entities mentioned on a webpage, our tool provides instant, detailed analysis that helps you understand content better.

Our Technology

EntitiesChecker uses state-of-the-art natural language processing models to identify entities such as people, organizations, locations, dates, and more. Our system not only recognizes these entities but also categorizes them and provides frequency analysis.

We've built our platform using a modern tech stack including Python, FastAPI, and advanced NLP libraries like spaCy. This combination allows us to process content quickly while maintaining high accuracy in entity recognition.
